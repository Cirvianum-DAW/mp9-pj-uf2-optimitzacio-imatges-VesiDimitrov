La Liga - Spain

La Liga is one of the most prestigious football leagues in Europe, captivating fans worldwide with its rich history, passionate fanbase, and world-class talent. As the top tier of professional football in Spain, La Liga showcases some of the best footballing talent in the world, with clubs like Real Madrid, Barcelona, and Atletico Madrid competing for glory.

  • Founded: 1929
  • Number of Teams: 20
  • Current Champions: Atletico Madrid
  • Most Titles: Real Madrid (34 titles)
